The Misfits

A comic loosely based on the Pokemon Dungeon Series. This rescue team is composed of Cheeze, Maudie, and Yasmayne. They go off doing silly adventures and helping other pokemon.

Characters

Not all the characters have names yet because I suck at them. This entire page is still under construction. I promise to write a more in-depth descriptions and stop it from being an eyesore eventually

Cheeze

The leader of The Rescue Team. Not much is know from her past since she tends to keep to herself. She won't admit it but she cares a lot about the team and often worries about letting them down.

Maudie

Princess who ran away in hopes to discover beauty and wonder. Life in the palace stop being comfortable after discovering the difficult life the citizens have. She asked Cheeze if she wanted to form a rescue team, offering to be the one carrying items and providing meals. She's a bit niave and often confused but her nanny has taught her to always be open minded.

Yasmayne

Cheeze's right hand man. She scans locations, prepares remedies, and annoys the hell out of Cheeze. She's clingy and lovesick but she gets the job done. She was abandoned at a young age due to an extreme tradition among Starlys. She cannot attack normally and relies heavily on seeds instead.

Carmen

She doesn't say anything but "yup".

Noctowl

Lesbian owl kleptomaniac. She is so real for this actually

Slugma

Hyperactive Princess. Maudie's pen pal :D

Xatu

Chief of the jungle and the steward of Mesprite (yes this story has the lake gaurdians because I can)
